Good to know

<div id="collapseFour" class="tab-content collapse" aria-labelledby="prod-good" data-parent="#accordion">
<p><strong>A natural material with a unique finish&nbsp;<br/></strong>As slate is a natural product each panel may have some slight impurities including veins, areas of discolouration and pyrite (fool's gold) deposits, making each piece of slate one of a kind.</p>
<p><strong>Easy to maintain&nbsp;<br/></strong>You shouldn&rsquo;t have to do anything with your house sign once it&rsquo;s been installed. However, if it does get dirty you can give it a quick clean with a damp cloth&hellip; don&rsquo;t worry about your sign details, they won&rsquo;t come off.</p>
<p><strong>Every house sign is unique&nbsp;<br/></strong>As this sign has a natural edge your design may be a millimetre or two from the perfect centre of the panel. Our quality control team check to ensure the sign looks correct and will reject anything they wouldn&rsquo;t be happy to receive themselves.</p>
<p><strong>Pre-drilled holes can chip the back of panel&nbsp;<br/></strong>Occasionally when we drill the fixing holes into the slate there is a possibility that the back of the panel can chip leaving an uneven surface. Rest assured, this has no effect on the appearance and integrity of the sign.</p>
